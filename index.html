Our Mission

Healing happens when barriers are removed.

From chemotherapy copays to travel costs for specialized surgery, we step in so families can focus on what matters: getting better. In 2025, we assisted over 9,200 patients across the country.

Core Programs

Where your kindness meets critical need.

💊

Treatment Grants

Direct financial aid for chemo, radiation, surgeries, and specialty medications. $500 covers a month of life-saving insulin for an uninsured patient.

🏠

Hope Lodges

Free housing near treatment centers for families traveling far from home. Over 340 families stayed with us last year, steps away from their child's hospital.

🧸

Emotional Care

Counseling, support groups, and activity kits for children battling long-term illness. A $100 donation sponsors a month of therapy for a young patient.
